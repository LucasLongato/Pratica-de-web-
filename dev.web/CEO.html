Harold Random

Random Records began it’s journey to success as a dream of a simple man, born and raised in West Virginia, Harold Random. From a very young age, Harold already showed an intense interest in all kinds of music. Despite being raised in a very strict family of mormons, Harold’s favourite music genre was progressive-metal (which, as you may imagine, wasn’t much popular in the Random household). When the age of 16 arrived, Harold decided that he wanted to change things for the better in his and everybody elses life, so he packed his bags and ran away from his home with only two socks, 3 hats, a old over-all and his trustworthy metal-banjo. With the sun on his face and his past behind him, the young man set-sail for the bright future ahead!
